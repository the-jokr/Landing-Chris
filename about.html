Our Solution
  • The app will bring in a community for jokes. Jokes have always been shared between others mostly by word of mouth.
  • DadJokes will be able to bring that aspect to a wider range of people through the ease of access in a social platform.
  • The app gives the users a reference for jokes when they need it so they can refer back to it.
The Mission Statement
  • Keep your favorite Dad jokes organized for quick access and sharing with your friends and family.
  • Spread the fun and laughter with your friends through your favorite Dad jokes well organized.
  • Share your Dad Jokes with everyone! Be corny, be funny but most of all be YOU. Join the fun at Dad Jokes!
App Features
  • Users are able to view public jokes.
  • Users can view a randomly generated joke.
  • Logged in users are able to add jokes into their Joke Wallet.
  • Users can share a joke that they like.
